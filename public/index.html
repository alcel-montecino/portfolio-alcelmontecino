Process

Estimated
project timeline

A typical full organizational website build — from kickoff to launch — takes 8–12 weeks depending on scope and content availability. Maintenance begins immediately after launch.

Week 1–2
Discovery & Architecture

Requirements gathering, site audit (if existing), tech stack confirmation, sitemap, and project plan. Review of fbcglenarden.org structure and desired functionality.

Week 3–4
Design & WordPress Setup

WordPress installation, custom theme development, responsive layout, typography, and color system. Mobile-first design reviewed and approved before build continues.

Week 5–7
Core Feature Development

Blog/CMS setup, Events Calendar with registration, Member Portal with role-based access, and Secure Online Giving integration (Stripe/PayPal/Pushpay).

Week 8–9
Integrations & Content Migration

Third-party platform connections (Mailchimp, Planning Center, streaming, social feeds), content migration from existing site, and media library setup.

Week 10–11
Testing & Optimization

Cross-browser and mobile testing, performance optimization, security hardening (SSL, OWASP), payment flow testing, and admin/staff training sessions.

Week 12+
Launch & Ongoing Maintenance

Site launch, DNS transition, monitoring setup, daily backups configured. Ongoing maintenance contract begins — regular updates, support, and continuous improvement.
